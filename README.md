## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs **Advanced Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on an E-Commerce Sales dataset provided during the DecodeLabs Data Analytics Internship.

The objective is to explore customer purchasing behavior, product performance, revenue generation, payment preferences, coupon usage, and customer segmentation to generate meaningful business insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Explore and understand the dataset.
- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing.
- Identify missing values and duplicate records.
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Evaluate product performance.
- Perform customer segmentation.
- Analyze payment methods and referral sources.
- Generate business recommendations.

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains information such as:

## 📈 Key Findings

- The dataset contains **1200 customer orders**.
- Coupon usage varies across customers.
- Customer segmentation identifies Premium, High, Medium, and Low-value customers.
- Certain products generate significantly higher revenue.
- Payment methods influence purchasing behavior.
- Referral sources contribute differently to overall sales.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Promote high-performing products.
- Improve coupon campaigns.
- Reward loyal customers.
- Invest in high-performing referral channels.
- Reduce cancelled orders.
- Increase average cart value using bundle offers.
